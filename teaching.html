Discover CS (EECS 198)

A new class that I designed and taught (as primary instructor), Discover CS is a one-credit freshmen-level class for students who are new to computer science to explore different areas of CS and learn basic programming skills.

  • We received funding from the Center for Research on Learning and Teaching (CRLT) Gilbert Whitaker Fund ($5,725)
  • Michigan Daily: "Introductory EECS course designed for women, those without prior experience embarks on first semester"
  • Department News: "New one-credit course allows those without experience to discover computer science"

  • Sample course materials: Programming Homework Assignment, Interview Assignment, Final Project Assignment
