I am a PhD pre-candidate in Computer Science and Engineering at the University of Michigan. I am a part of the LIT research group, supervised by Dr. Rada Mihalcea.

My research interests include using natural language processing and machine learning to better understand people's behaviors and language. I am interested in the intersection of computer science and social science.

Outreach

I care deeply about helping younger women succeed in computer science. Towards that end, I am currently one of the organizers for CS KickStart, a week-long summer program designed to help women explore computer science. I am also involved in the Girls Encoded initiative at the University of Michigan.
